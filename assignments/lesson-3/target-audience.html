Website Planning Document- Target Audience

DESCRIPTION

Age: all age groups (excluding young children, below the age of 10)
Income: average household income (which is about $60,000 annually)
Education: on 'average GED or a high school diploma minimum
Experience with the Topic: minimal, but basic background knowledge and interest
Family Status: any familial status (ex. single, married, parents, children, etc.)

PERSONAS

  1. Jane Doe-
    • Demographics and Education: 32 year old, stay at home wife and mother of 3 children (11, 8, 5 years old); general college associate’s degree
    • Goals and Motivation for Using the Site: keep up to date about community meetings and activities in the town where she lives
    • Social: active in her community and church, enjoys spending time with her friend who have children similar in age to her own, love spending time doing outdoor activities with her husband and children
    • Technology: primarily uses the family desktop PC, owns an iPhone that she uses mainly when she is away from home, also owns an iPad, but far less often than other devices
  2. Johnny Smith-
    • Demographics and Education: 15 year old, high school student from a single parent home; average grades and scheduled to graduate in three years
    • Goals and Motivation for Using the Site: completing a school project where he tracks the daily weather of his town for two months
    • Social: enjoys participating in extracurricular sports, including baseball and track and field, loves spending time outdoors with his friends on the weekend archery hunting and fishing
    • Technology: he uses MAC laptops that are at the school and also has a iPhone that he uses whenever he isn’t at school

SCENARIOS

  1. What is the purpose of communityweather.us?
  2. What information (other than the weather) can I find about my community on communityweather.us?
  3. Where can I find emergency weather warnings for all the towns in one place?
  4. What bonus material is included on the site, other than weather and community news?
  5. How can I request information be posted to communityweather.us?
